Summary: Latitude vs. [Different Climate Factors]

                
                
                                         
                    

I analyzed the different climate factors that affects close to 550 different cities in the entire world. I used matplotlib to plot the different climate factors vs. the latitude. The factors are max temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed.
