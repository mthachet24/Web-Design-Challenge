Cloudiness Visualization

                
                
                                         
                    

I compared the different variables: latitude and Cloudiness together and created a plot of it regarding the cities in the csv file. The plot shows that there isn't really much of a relationship between the two.

For example, if there is a latitude of 40, there is a cloudiness % ranging from 100% all the way to 0%. This is the case for all of the latitude points across the entire plot.

There seems to be more 100% cloudiness at a latitude ranging from 0 to 30 and 40 to 75.
