Humidity Visualization

                
                
                                         
                    

I compared the different variables: latitude and Humidity together and created a plot of it regarding the cities in the csv file. The plot shows that the majority of the plot has points plotted between a humidity percentage of 60% to 100%.

The smaller majority of the points plotted were under the Humidity range of 60% to 0%.

The threshold seems to be at 60% for humidity when it comes to having a high humidity or low humidity above or below 60%.
